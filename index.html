Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how VoiceLink ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Device identifiers for connection purposes
  • Network information for device discovery
  • Audio recordings (stored locally only)
  • Text messages (stored locally only)

Technical Information

  • Device model and operating system version
  • App version and usage statistics
  • Network connectivity status

How We Use Your Information

  • Enable voice and text communication between devices
  • Maintain connection stability and quality
  • Provide technical support and troubleshooting
  • Improve app performance and user experience

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage Only

All your messages, voice recordings, and personal data are stored locally on your device. We do not upload, store, or access your personal communications on external servers.

  • End-to-end encryption for all communications
  • No cloud storage of personal data
  • Secure local database with encryption
  • Regular security updates and patches

Data Sharing

We Do Not Share Your Data

VoiceLink does not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Your communications remain private between connected devices.
